BLDC Token Account Deletion Request

How to Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your account, please follow these steps:

You can send a deletion request email to bldctoken@gmail.com.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently deleted from our system:

  • Transaction history
  • Referral information
  • Staking data

Any data required for legal compliance or that must be retained for a specific period (e.g., for financial reporting) will be kept for the duration required by law.

Data Retention Period

Certain data, such as transaction records, may be retained for up to 5 years to comply with financial regulations.

Confirmation

Once your account deletion request is processed, you will receive a confirmation email, and all your data will be permanently deleted. Please note that this process may take up to 7 business days.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about account deletion, please contact us at: bldctoken@gmail.com
